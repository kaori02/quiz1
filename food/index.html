The Taste of Jambi

Tempoyak

Tempoyak is a typical Jambi food which is also popular in various other regions in Sumatra.

Tempoyak has food ingredients in the form of fermented durian. Generally, Tempoyak is served together with white rice.

Apart from being eaten directly, this Jambi special food can also be used as a spice in cooking as a flavoring for other foods. It's no wonder that many tourists bring them for souvenirs.

Gulai Tepek

Gulai Tepek Ikan is a delicious dish from Jambi. As a typical Jambi traditional dish, Gulai Tepek Ikan is often served at traditional events such as weddings and religious events.

This Jambi traditional culinary is a combination of fish tepek which at first glance looks like Palembang pempek combined with curry sauce, creating a rich flavor of spices in the style of Indonesian cuisine.

Pempek Sambal

Of course, almost everyone in Indonesia knows pempek, with its distinctive vinegar sauce. However, there is something different from this pempek variant, namely the sauce uses chili sauce.

Mixed with lime leaves and turmeric leaves, this Jambi traditional dish is Jambi's answer to Pempek!

Gandus

The distinctive white color of this cake comes from rice flour, sago flour, and salt plus coconut milk.

This talam ebi-like cake is usually served in a square or bowl mold. The sprinkling of fried onions on top makes this cake taste delicious.
